# Product Overview

## Triathlon Team Website

A modern React-based website for a triathlon team featuring:

- **Main landing page** - Team introduction and overview
- **Coaches page** - Meet the coaching staff and their expertise
- **Contact page** - Get in touch with the team
- **Events page** - Upcoming races, training sessions, and team events
- **Velocity Cycling Classes** - Registration system for specialized cycling training

The site serves both current team members and prospective athletes looking to join the triathlon community. Focus on clean, athletic design with easy navigation and mobile responsiveness.

## Target Users
- Current team members seeking information and class registration
- Prospective athletes interested in joining
- Coaches managing events and classes
- General public interested in triathlon training
